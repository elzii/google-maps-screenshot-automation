## Google Maps Scaling 

Google Map Tiles - 256px
140 PPI

(256/100) * (2.54) = 6.5cm
                   = 0.065m

(256/140) * (2.54) = 4.65cm
                   = 0.0465m

At zoom level 0, entire 360 deg are visible at equator

360deg = 40,075.16km = 40075160m

(40075160m/0.065m) = 616313361

Zoom level 1 = one half of that of zoom level 0
Zoom level N = 0.5(N-1)
